Frequently Asked Questions

  • How does payment work?

    You have the option to make a one-time payment via PayPal or WebDollar.

  • Can I get a multi-seat license?

    At the moment, only one plan for each account is allowed. You can create multiple accounts or choose a bigger plan.

  • What do you mean by ‘Unlimited’?

    There is an maximum hard limit set for accounts. You can create up to 500 miners per account. If you have more miners, please contact us and we can create a custom setup.

  • Do you support custom plans?

    Yes, write us an email and we can set up your account with a custom plan.

  • Is this a subscription?

    You can create subscriptions via PayPal.

  • Can I cancel at any time?

    Yes, no questions asked :)

Did we miss something?

Feel free to drop us a line with any further questions. One of our support team will get back to you ASAP.
