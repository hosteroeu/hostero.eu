Instructions

You need to have an account to mine POS with WebDollar. If you don't already have one, go to Sign Up.

1. Go to Devices

Go to Devices and locate the WebDollar POS device.

2. Deploy Miner

Click on the + (plus) button, on the right side of the device, and enter your WebDollar.io wallet, then click Deploy.
